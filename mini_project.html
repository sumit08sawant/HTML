About MCA

The Mumbai Cricket Association is the governing body for cricket in Mumbai and its surrounding regions like Thane and Navi Mumbai. The jurisdiction of MCA includes the area up to Dahanu in the Western Suburbs, Badlapur in the Central Suburbs, and Navi Mumbai up to Kharghar. The association comes under the west zone.
